Tandoorie Chicken
₹250

Tandoori chicken is a popular Indian dish marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices, including cumin, coriander, ginger, and garam masala. Traditionally cooked in a tandoor (clay oven), it emerges with a vibrant red hue and a smoky flavor. The yogurt tenderizes the chicken while the spices infuse it with rich, aromatic notes, resulting in a succulent and flavorful dish enjoyed worldwide.

Sichuan Cuisine
₹300

Sichuan cuisine, also known as Szechuan cuisine, is a renowned culinary style hailing from the Sichuan province of China. Characterized by its bold and spicy flavors, Sichuan cuisine is famous for its use of garlic, chili peppers, Sichuan peppercorns, and a variety of other aromatic spices. It offers a harmonious balance of flavors, combining numbing, spicy, sweet, and sour elements to create distinctive dishes such as Mapo tofu, Kung Pao chicken, and Sichuan hot pot. The cuisine's rich history and unique flavors have made it immensely popular both within China and internationally, earning it a well-deserved reputation as one of the most beloved regional cuisines in the world.

Pav Bhaji
₹100

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food dish originating from Mumbai. It consists of a flavorful mashed vegetable curry (bhaji) made with potatoes, tomatoes, peas, and spices, served with buttered and toasted buns (pav). The bhaji is typically cooked with a blend of spices like pav bhaji masala, giving it a rich and spicy flavor. It's often garnished with chopped onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice for added freshness. This hearty and satisfying dish is loved for its bold flavors and comforting warmth.

Spicy Italian Sausage Pizza

Spicy Italian Sausage Pizza is a tantalizing combination of zesty flavors. It features a crispy crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and slices of savory Italian sausage. The spiciness of the sausage adds a kick that perfectly complements the richness of the cheese and the sweetness of the sauce, creating a mouthwatering culinary experience that satisfies any pizza lover's cravings.

Hamburger
₹150

A hamburger is a classic American dish consisting of a seasoned ground beef patty, typically cooked on a grill or skillet, served on a bun. It's often accompanied by various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, and condiments like ketchup and mustard. This iconic comfort food is known for its simplicity yet satisfying combination of flavors and textures, making it a beloved choice for casual dining around the world.

Butter Chicken
₹250

Butter chicken is a popular Indian dish made with tender pieces of chicken cooked in a creamy, tomato-based sauce. It's seasoned with a blend of aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala, then finished with a rich swirl of butter and cream for a smooth, indulgent flavor. Served hot with rice or naan, it's a savory and satisfying dish that's beloved for its creamy texture and spicy-sweet taste..
